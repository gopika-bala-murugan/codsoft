Chithirai Thiruvizha: Celebrating Divine Union in Madurai

Chithirai Thiruvizha is a sacred festival in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, honoring the celestial union of Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. Lasting for days, it features a vibrant procession where devotees accompany elaborately adorned idols through the city's streets. This joyous celebration, filled with music, prayers, and cultural performances, draws crowds from far and wide, encapsulating the deep-rooted spiritual and cultural essence of Madurai.
